The **Queen DeFAI Fund** is an AI-powered investment vehicle that is trading AI Agent tokens and interacts with various DeFi protocols to optimize yield generation. The **AI Agent QUEEN** autonomously manages assets, reallocating liquidity across protocols to maximize returns. A portion of the generated yield is distributed to **QUEEN stakers**, further incentivizing long-term participation and reinforcing the flywheel effect.
