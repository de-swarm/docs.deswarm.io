## Token-Gated Terminal

The **Token-Gated Terminal** serves as the primary interface for users to interact with QUEEN, providing real-time DeFi insights. Access to the terminal is restricted to holders of a minimum threshold of $QUEEN tokens, ensuring exclusive usage for committed participants.

### Features

1. **AI-Driven Market Insights**
    - Users receive real-time market analysis powered by QUEEN’s models
    - AI identifies trading opportunities based on sentiment analysis, price trends, and on-chain activity
2. **DeFi Assistance** 
    - QUEEN provides insights on current DeFi environment regarding:
        - Current APY among various protocols
        - Liquidity Pools
        - Details on users’ activity
        - DeFi news coming from different data sources
        - Highest Yield strategies for particular tokens of choice
3. **Sentiment Analysis & Market Intelligence**
    - QUEEN aggregates and analyzes data from social media, news platforms, and on-chain metrics
    - Provides sentiment scores on tokens and market trends to guide user decisions
4. **Trade Execution & Recommendations**
    - Users can input trade ideas in natural language, which QUEEN translates into executable on-chain strategies
    - AI suggests whether to buy, sell, or hold assets based on risk-adjusted analysis

The **Token-Gated Terminal** is the gateway for users to leverage QUEEN’s intelligence, allowing them to optimize their DeFAI strategies with minimal manual effort.
