## **QUEEN API**

The **QUEEN API** will provide access to **AI-driven insights, on-chain analytics, and optimized DeFi strategies**, making it a powerful tool for developers, traders, and protocols. 

Leveraging vast datasets, machine learning models, and live blockchain data, QUEEN will offer intelligent decision-making capabilities to those integrating it into their systems.

To ensure alignment with the ecosystem, access to the **QUEEN API** will be granted to projects who **airdrop a portion of their supply** to **QUEEN stakers**. This mechanism incentivizes adoption while rewarding long-term supporters, reinforcing the **flywheel** of the QUEEN token.
