DeSwarm is a Trading & Yield Optimization Platform Powered by AI, enabling users to maximize returns through high-yield automated vaults, trade perpetuals on a native perps DEX, and utilize a terminal for market sentiment analysis and DeFi insights—all within the Berachain ecosystem.

Our system leverages AI to process market data and execute strategies through seamless integrations with DeFi protocols on Berachain. DeSwarm’s modular stack allows for the integration of new protocols and data sources, continuously improving through reinforcement learning based on trading outcomes and market conditions.

**Core Components:**

- **DeFAI Terminal**: A search engine for real-time DeFi insights and sentiment analysis, helping users identify the best opportunities on Berachain
- **DeFAI Vaults**: AI-driven vaults that optimize yield by dynamically allocating liquidity across Berachain protocols
- **Perpetual DEX**: A native decentralized exchange for trading perpetuals
- **Proof of Liquidity Integration**: DeSwarm connects with Berachain’s Proof of Liquidity (PoL) to enhance rewards for `$DSM` stakers and DeSwarm Vaults users through BGT emissions and platform fees

**Launch**: Q2 2025
