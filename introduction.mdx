DeSwarm is a Yield Optimatization Platform Powered by AI Agents Swarm that enables users to interact with AI Agent QUEEN, ape into high-yield vaults, and launch autonomous on-chain funds.

Our system processes market data to execute trades through DeFi protocols integrations.

Modular stack, allowing seamless integration of new protocols and data sources. The system improves through reinforcement learning on trading outcomes and market data.

Core components: autonomous DeFAI agent managing funds and a token-gated terminal.&#x20;

***Launch: Q1 2025.***
